About Me

What it does?

The Hennessey Venom F5 is a supercar with a top speed of 301 mph (485 km/h). It is powered by a 7.4-litre V8 engine and boasts a 0-60 mph acceleration time of under 2 seconds. Reference Link

How does it do it?

Venom F5 uses rear-wheel drive, via a single-clutch automated seven-speed manual. Without all-wheel drive and a heavy dual-clutch transmission the Venom F5 is able to tread very lightly on the road, with a kerb weight of just 1338kg. Reference Link

Why buy it?

Hennessey projects a 0-300km/h time of below 10 seconds, and a 0-400-0km/h time of under 30 seconds. These will be some incredible numbers if they turn out to be accurate in the real world. Reference Link
